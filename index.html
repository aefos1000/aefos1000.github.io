ABOUT MEAlexandra (Ally) Fosco is a Graphic Designer/ Illustrator from Plymouth, MA. She is a versatile designer with training in all of the Adobe programs, as well as having a rich background in illustration techniques. She is a sophomore student at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth CVPA, and is majoring in Graphic Design, and minoring in Illustration. Some of her favorite things to design are based around nature such as flowers, animals, and assorted plants as well. Coming from an illustration background, her design work is focused on optimizing the look of sketch, but is not limited to that. Her dream is to become a tattoo artist to create balance between the design and Illustration field. Creating art for people to view and feel a connection to is something she holds greatly to her heart
